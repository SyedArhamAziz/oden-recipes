Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cream
  • Ice
  • Salt
  • Flavourings (optional)

Procedure

  1. Mix 2 parts milk to 1 part cream into a bowl
  2. Put that bowl into a larger bowl filled with ice and salt (as salt decreases the freezing temp of water)
  3. Mix with an electric beater until the mixture begins to firm up
  4. Add any flavourings you want at this stage
  5. Put mixture into freezer for at least 2 hours, but up to a day if possible
  6. Take mixture out and mix it again
  7. Your icecream is now ready to be served. It will be closer to soft-serve in consistency so keep that in mind for applications
