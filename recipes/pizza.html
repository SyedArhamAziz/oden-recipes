Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Toppings (whatever you prefer)

Procedure

  1. Preheat the oven to 410F or 210C (adding a pizza steel or stone if you have one
  2. Knead your dough with water until it is strong enough to stretch. Avoid adding excess flour
  3. Roll your dough out into a rough circular shape, keeping the outside thicker than the center
  4. Add on a thin layer of tomato sauce
  5. Add on your cheese(s). If you're going to add on Parmesan add it before the mozzarella
  6. You may now add your toppings, preferably sliced thin to ensure even cooking
  7. Put your pizza on a baking pan which has been lightly greased to prevent sticking, or directly on your pan/steel
  8. Wait around 20 minutes for you pizza to cook. Take it out once the cheese just starts to brown, or else it will separate
  9. Take the pizza out of the oven and let it rest for at least 20 minutes on a wire rack before cutting
  10. Slice the pizza and enjoy!
